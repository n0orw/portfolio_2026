About Me

I'm a freelance video editor and motion graphics designer obsessed with creating content that doesn't just look good—it performs.

Every frame matters. Whether it's a 15-second Instagram Reel or a full brand commercial, I approach each project with the same goal: capture attention in the first second and hold it until the last. My background in storytelling, visual aesthetics, and platform-specific editing helps brands stand out in crowded feeds and turn viewers into customers.

I work with cafés, restaurants, startups, and local businesses who need high-quality video content without the agency overhead. Fast turnarounds, clear communication, and results-driven creativity—that's what you get when we collaborate.
